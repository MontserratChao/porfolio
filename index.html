About me

I have dedicated most of my life to drawing, which I still do, while I have messed around with different things such as; at the time of 2015 when Tumblr was at its peak, I started messing around with HTML and CSS codes discovering how entertaining it is. Which led me to do a computer science degree where I was only interested in the little programming they gave. After that course, I left aside a little and the otaku and geek part of me (that I still conserve tho) to enter the infamous fandom of: kpop. Where I not only discovered many varied artists that I admire, to whom I have drawn and dedicated projects (as you can see above)... but it also made me interested in somethings that I didn't think I would end up studying and dedicating, which was design. I began to be interested in the design of the albums that I have bought, of the visual teasers, videos and other concepts that made me see everything with different eyes. And so I joined something that has always entertained and liked such as web design and even APPs as something that I have just discovered, such as design, which in a certain way I have always been close to as an artist.
